<p><strong>The PCB Soldering Oven Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wave Soldering Oven</li><li>Reflow Soldering Oven</li><li>Selective Soldering Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB soldering oven market consists of three main types: wave soldering ovens, reflow soldering ovens, and selective soldering ovens. Wave soldering ovens are primarily used for through-hole components, employing a wave of molten solder to efficiently join connections. Reflow soldering ovens cater to surface-mount technology, using controlled heat to melt solder paste and create strong bonds. Selective soldering ovens target specific areas for soldering, ideal for mixed technology boards. Each type serves distinct applications in the electronics manufacturing process.</p></p>
