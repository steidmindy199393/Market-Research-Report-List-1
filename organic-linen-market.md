<p><p>The organic linen market comprises three main types: dry spun yarn, wet spun yarn, and blended yarn. Dry spun yarn involves twisting and spinning fiber into yarn without moisture, resulting in a strong, durable product ideal for home textiles. Wet spun yarn, on the other hand, uses moisture in its production, offering a softer texture, commonly utilized in finer garments. Blended yarn combines organic linen with other fibers, enhancing versatility and performance in various applications, appealing to a broader consumer base.</p></p>
