<p><p>Military aircraft actuation systems are critical for controlling flight surfaces, landing gear, and other mechanisms. Hydraulic actuators use pressurized fluid for high force and reliability, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Electric actuators rely on electric motors, offering precision and reduced maintenance, suitable for modern, lightweight designs. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air, providing simplicity and quick response, often used in secondary systems. Each type caters to specific operational needs, balancing performance, weight, and complexity in military aviation.</p></p>
