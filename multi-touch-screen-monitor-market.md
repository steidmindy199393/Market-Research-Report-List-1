<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Resistive</li><li>Sonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-touch screen monitor market is categorized into three main types: capacitive, resistive, and sonic. Capacitive screens detect touch through electrical signals, offering enhanced sensitivity and clarity, making them ideal for smartphones and tablets. Resistive screens, composed of layers that register pressure, are more durable and cost-effective, suitable for industrial applications. Sonic screens use sound waves to detect touch, providing a contactless experience, which is advantageous in environments requiring hygiene or where physical touch is impractical.</p></p>
