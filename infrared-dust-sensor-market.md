<p><p>The Infrared Dust Sensor market is segmented primarily into types based on output configurations: UART Output and PWM Output. UART Output sensors utilize serial communication protocols to transmit dust concentration data, ensuring high accuracy and efficiency in data transfer, making them suitable for advanced applications. In contrast, PWM Output sensors use pulse-width modulation to represent the dust levels, providing a simpler interface for basic applications. Both types are essential for various industries, including air quality monitoring and environmental research.</p></p>
