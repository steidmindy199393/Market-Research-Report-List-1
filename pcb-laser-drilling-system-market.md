<p><strong>The PCB Laser Drilling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CO2 Laser Drilling System</li><li>UV Laser Drilling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB laser drilling system market comprises two primary types: CO2 laser drilling systems and UV laser drilling systems. CO2 laser drilling systems utilize a carbon dioxide laser, ideal for creating larger holes in thicker substrates, commonly used in standard PCB applications. Conversely, UV laser drilling systems employ ultraviolet light, offering higher precision for microvias and intricate designs, making them suitable for advanced applications in high-density interconnect (HDI) PCBs. Both systems enhance production efficiency and accuracy in electronic manufacturing.</p></p>
