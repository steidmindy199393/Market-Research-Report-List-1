<p><p>Flat heat pipes are essential thermal management solutions, available primarily in two market types: ultra-thin and standard. Ultra-thin heat pipes are designed to offer maximum efficiency in compact spaces, making them ideal for lightweight electronics and mobile devices. In contrast, standard heat pipes provide a robust solution for larger applications, such as computer cooling systems and industrial equipment. Both types utilize the same capillary action principle but cater to different requirements based on size, heat capacity, and application specificity.</p></p>
