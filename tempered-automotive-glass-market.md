<p><p>The tempered automotive glass market includes windscreens, backlites, sidelites, and sunroofs, each serving specific functions. Windscreens provide front visibility and safety, often laminated for shatter resistance. Backlites, or rear windows, offer rear visibility and structural support. Sidelites, or side windows, enhance side visibility and are typically tempered for safety. Sunroofs, made of tempered or laminated glass, allow light and ventilation while maintaining vehicle integrity. All types prioritize durability, safety, and clarity, catering to diverse automotive design and performance needs.</p></p>
