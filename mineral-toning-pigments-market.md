<p><p>The mineral toning pigments market is divided into two main types: natural pigments and synthetic pigments. Natural pigments are derived from organic sources, such as rocks and minerals, offering eco-friendly options with rich color properties. They are often preferred for their aesthetic appeal and lower environmental impact. In contrast, synthetic pigments are chemically manufactured for consistent color quality and a wide range of hues. These pigments often provide better stability and durability, catering to various applications in industries like cosmetics and coatings.</p></p>
