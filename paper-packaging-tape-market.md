<p><ul><li>Adhesive Paper Packaging Tape</li><li>Non-Adhesive Paper Packaging Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper packaging tape market is primarily divided into two types: adhesive and non-adhesive paper packaging tape. Adhesive paper packaging tape features a sticky backing that allows it to securely adhere to surfaces, making it ideal for sealing boxes and packages. In contrast, non-adhesive paper packaging tape does not have a sticky surface and is typically used for bundling, strapping, or reinforcing items, relying on mechanical fasteners or other methods for attachment. Both types cater to sustainable packaging needs.</p></p>
