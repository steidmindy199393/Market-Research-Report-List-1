<p><p>The insulated windows market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Secondary Glazing** adds a second layer of glass to existing windows for improved insulation.</p><p>2. **Tinted Glass** reduces solar heat gain and glare by incorporating color into the glass.</p><p>3. **Double and Triple Glazing** involves multiple glass layers with air or gas-filled spaces for enhanced thermal efficiency.</p><p>4. **Low-Emissivity Coating Glazing** minimizes heat transfer while allowing light in. </p><p>5. **Reflective Coating Glazing** reflects solar radiation, helping maintain interior temperatures.</p></p>
