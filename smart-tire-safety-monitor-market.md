<p><strong>The Smart Tire Safety Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Interior Monitor</li><li>External Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Tire Safety Monitor market includes two main types: Interior Monitors and External Monitors. Interior Monitors are installed inside the tire, providing real-time data on pressure, temperature, and wear. They are highly accurate and durable but require professional installation. External Monitors attach to the tire's valve stem, offering similar data but are easier to install and maintain. Both types enhance safety by alerting drivers to potential tire issues, improving fuel efficiency, and extending tire lifespan.</p></p>
