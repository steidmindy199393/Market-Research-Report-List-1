<p><ul><li>Dry-set Mortar</li><li>Latex-portland Cement Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thinset mortar market comprises primarily two types: dry-set mortar and latex-portland cement mortar. Dry-set mortar is a cement-based adhesive that requires mixing with water, ideal for indoor applications due to its strong bonding properties. Conversely, latex-portland cement mortar incorporates latex additives, enhancing flexibility and water resistance, making it suitable for use in wet areas or exterior applications. These two types cater to various installation needs in the tile and flooring industry, emphasizing performance and durability.</p></p>
