<p><ul><li>Microwave RF Repeaters</li><li>Linear RF Repeaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF repeater system market is primarily categorized into microwave RF repeaters and linear RF repeaters. Microwave RF repeaters operate at higher frequencies, typically within the microwave spectrum, ensuring long-distance signal transmission with minimal loss, making them ideal for mobile networks and satellite communications. Linear RF repeaters, on the other hand, amplify signals without altering their waveform, suitable for various applications including broadcast and telecommunications, enabling clear and reliable communication over longer distances. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing signal strength and coverage.</p></p>
