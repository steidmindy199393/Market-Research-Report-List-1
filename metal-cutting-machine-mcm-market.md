<p><p>The Metal Cutting Machine (MCM) market encompasses various technologies used for precision metal fabrication. Laser cutting machines utilize focused beams of light for intricate cuts, while waterjet cutting machines employ high-pressure water mixed with abrasives for versatile cutting on various materials. Plasma cutting machines use ionized gas to melt and sever metal, whereas flame cutting machines burn through metal with a high-temperature flame. Other methods include mechanical and electrical cutting techniques, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
