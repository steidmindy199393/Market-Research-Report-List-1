<p><ul><li>Active Power Electric Power Meters</li><li>Reactive Power Electric Power Meters</li><li>Apparent Power Electric Power Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric power meters market comprises three main types: Active Power Meters, Reactive Power Meters, and Apparent Power Meters. Active Power Meters measure real power consumed by electrical devices, reflecting actual energy usage. Reactive Power Meters assess power that does not perform useful work, important in inductive loads, such as motors. Apparent Power Meters quantify the total power in an AC circuit, combining both active and reactive power, providing a holistic view of energy consumption and efficiency in electrical systems.</p></p>
