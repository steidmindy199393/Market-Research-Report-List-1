<p><p>The IoT motion sensor market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Passive Infrared (PIR)** sensors detect changes in infrared radiation due to motion, making them energy-efficient for indoor and outdoor use.</p><p>2. **Microwave sensors** emit microwave signals to sense motion, offering greater detection range and penetration through obstacles.</p><p>3. **Ultrasound sensors** use sound waves at frequencies higher than human hearing to detect movement, suitable for various applications.</p><p>4. **Tomography sensors** create detailed three-dimensional images of motion, useful in complex environments.</p><p>5. **Other sensors** encompass various emerging technologies tailored for specific requirements.</p></p>
