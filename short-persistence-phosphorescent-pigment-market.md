<p><p>Short Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment is a type of luminescent material that absorbs and stores light energy, emitting it gradually over a short period. Unlike long-persistence phosphors, these pigments glow for a shorter duration, typically minutes, making them ideal for applications requiring temporary visibility or aesthetic effects. They are widely used in industries such as textiles, paints, coatings, cosmetics, and safety signage due to their unique light-emitting properties and versatility.</p><p>The Short Persistence Phosphorescent Pigment Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period.
