<p><p>Reid Vapor Pressure (RVP) analyzers measure the volatility of liquid petroleum products by assessing the pressure exerted by vapor above a liquid at a standardized temperature. The market is categorized into standard pressure and high-pressure analyzers. Standard pressure analyzers focus on lower vapor pressures typical for gasoline and similar fuels, while high-pressure analyzers are designed for substances with higher vapor pressures, such as certain crude oils and gasolines. Both types are essential for quality control and regulatory compliance in the petroleum industry.</p></p>
