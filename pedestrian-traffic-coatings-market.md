<p><p>Pedestrian traffic coatings are essential for providing durable and slip-resistant surfaces in high-footfall areas. The market is primarily divided into two types: one-component polyurethane and two-component polyurethane coatings. One-component polyurethane coatings are user-friendly, requiring no mixing and offering quick application and curing. In contrast, two-component polyurethane coatings involve mixing a resin and hardener, resulting in superior durability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for more demanding environments. Both types cater to various pedestrian applications, ensuring safety and longevity.</p></p>
