<p><p>The Exhaust Catalyst Market includes Tightly Coupled Catalysts and Bottom Tray Catalysts, both designed to reduce vehicle emissions. Tightly Coupled Catalysts are positioned close to the engine, enabling faster activation and efficient emission control during cold starts. Bottom Tray Catalysts are located further downstream, typically integrated into the exhaust system, offering durability and effective pollutant conversion. Both types play crucial roles in meeting stringent emission standards, with Tightly Coupled Catalysts excelling in quick response and Bottom Tray Catalysts in long-term performance.</p></p>
