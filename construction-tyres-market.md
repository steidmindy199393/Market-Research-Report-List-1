<p><strong>The Construction Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Ply</li><li>Radial Ply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction tyres market primarily comprises two types: bias ply and radial ply tyres. Bias ply tyres have layers of nylon or fabric that crisscross at angles, offering durability and a soft ride, making them suitable for rough terrains. In contrast, radial ply tyres feature steel belts and are designed with a more flexible sidewall, providing better traction, stability, and fuel efficiency. Both types cater to various construction equipment, with preferences varying based on operational needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
