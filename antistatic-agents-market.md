<p><ul><li>Cationic Antistic Agent</li><li>Anionic Antistatic Agent</li><li>Non-ionic Antistatic Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antistatic agents market is categorized into three main types: cationic, anionic, and non-ionic antistatic agents. Cationic agents carry a positive charge, effectively neutralizing negatively charged surfaces, while anionic agents are negatively charged, suitable for applications where they can adhere to positively charged materials. Non-ionic antistatic agents, lacking a charge, offer versatile compatibility with various substrates and environmental conditions. Each type is utilized across diverse industries, including plastics, textiles, and electronics, to control static electricity and enhance product performance.</p></p>
