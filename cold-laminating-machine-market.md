<p><p>A cold laminating machine is a device used to apply a protective layer of laminate film onto various surfaces without the use of heat. It works by utilizing pressure to bond the laminate to the substrate, providing durability and protection against moisture, UV rays, and physical wear. These machines are commonly employed in industries like printing, signage, and packaging, as well as by hobbyists for preserving documents and photos.</p><p>The Cold Laminating Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.1% during the forecast period. Key trends include the movement toward eco-friendly laminating solutions and advances in technology, such as automated features and enhanced user interfaces.
