<p><p>Smart power sockets are categorized primarily into three market types: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and other technologies. Bluetooth sockets offer local control, ideal for short-range connectivity, and are often used for quick pairing with smartphones. Other market types encompass variations using technologies like Zigbee or Z-Wave, enhancing compatibility with smart home ecosystems. Each type caters to different user needs and preferences for connectivity and automation.</p></p>
