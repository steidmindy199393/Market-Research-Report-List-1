<p><p>Railway signaling equipment encompasses various advanced technologies for train control and safety. The Communication-Based Train Control (CBTC) system enhances real-time communication between trains and track infrastructure, optimizing traffic flow and safety. Positive Train Control (PTC) integrates GPS, wireless radio, and database systems to prevent accidents and enforce speed limits. Automatic Train Control (ATC) automates throttle and braking functions, ensuring trains operate within safe parameters. Together, these technologies improve efficiency, reduce delays, and enhance overall railway safety.</p></p>
