<p><p>An Anti-Lock Braking Device (ABS) is a safety system in vehicles that prevents wheels from locking up during braking, ensuring better control and stability. It works by automatically modulating brake pressure, allowing the driver to steer while braking, especially on slippery surfaces. ABS is now a standard feature in most modern vehicles, including cars, motorcycles, and commercial vehicles, due to its ability to reduce accidents and improve road safety.</p><p>The Anti-Lock Braking Device Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing vehicle production, stringent safety regulations, and rising consumer awareness about road safety. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.9% during the forecast period. This growth is fueled by advancements in automotive technology, such as the integration of ABS with electronic stability control (ESC) and other advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). Additionally, the rising demand for electric vehicles (EVs) and autonomous vehicles is contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Emerging trends in the ABS market include the development of lightweight and compact systems, the adoption of sensor fusion technology, and the increasing use of ABS in two-wheelers, particularly in developing regions. Asia-Pacific is a key growth region due to rapid urbanization, increasing disposable incomes, and growing automotive sales. Overall, the ABS market is poised for robust growth, driven by technological innovation and the global push for safer transportation.</p></p>
