<p><p>The KW-Class High Power Fiber Lasers market consists of two primary types: Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Lasers and Pulsed Fiber Lasers. CW Fiber Lasers provide a constant output of laser light, ideal for applications requiring consistent and stable energy delivery, such as cutting and welding. In contrast, Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit energy in short bursts, making them suitable for precision applications, including marking, engraving, and medical procedures. Both types are integral in various industrial and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
