<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Type</li><li>Mechanical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vertical Rubber Injection Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Hydraulic and Mechanical. Hydraulic machines utilize pressurized fluid to create force for the injection process, offering high efficiency and precision, making them suitable for complex molds and larger production runs. In contrast, Mechanical machines rely on mechanical components, such as screws and gears, for the injection mechanism, providing faster operation and lower initial costs. Both types serve specific production needs in the rubber manufacturing industry.</p></p>
