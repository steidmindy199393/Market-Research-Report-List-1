<p><strong>The Manual Steering Rack Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack and Pinion Type</li><li>Ball Screw Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual steering rack market primarily includes two types: Rack and Pinion and Ball Screw. Rack and Pinion systems use a gear mechanism to convert rotational motion into linear motion, offering precise control and simplicity, making them common in passenger vehicles. Ball Screw systems utilize ball bearings to reduce friction, providing smoother operation and higher efficiency, often used in heavy-duty or high-performance applications. Both types aim to enhance steering responsiveness and driver comfort, catering to different vehicle requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
