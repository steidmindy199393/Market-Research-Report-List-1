<p><p>The rear axle market includes Lift Axles, Dead Axles, and Drive Axles, each serving distinct purposes. Lift Axles are retractable, enhancing load capacity when needed and improving fuel efficiency when raised. Dead Axles are non-powered, supporting weight without driving the vehicle, often used in trailers. Drive Axles transmit power from the engine to the wheels, crucial for propulsion in trucks and heavy vehicles. These axles cater to varying operational needs, balancing load management, efficiency, and performance in commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
