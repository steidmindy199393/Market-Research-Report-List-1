<p><p>The semiconductor test equipment market is primarily divided into two types: wafer test equipment and packaged device test equipment. Wafer test equipment is used to assess the performance and reliability of semiconductor wafers before they are diced into individual chips. In contrast, packaged device test equipment is designed to evaluate completed semiconductor devices, checking their functionality and adherence to specifications. Both types are crucial for ensuring high-quality semiconductor manufacturing, driving advancements in technology, and meeting industry standards.</p></p>
