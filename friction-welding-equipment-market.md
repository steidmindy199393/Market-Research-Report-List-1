<p><strong>The Friction Welding Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Friction Welding</li><li>Linear Friction Welding</li><li>Friction Stir Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The friction welding equipment market encompasses three primary types: Rotary Friction Welding, Linear Friction Welding, and Friction Stir Welding. Rotary Friction Welding involves rotating one workpiece against another, generating heat through friction to join materials. Linear Friction Welding uses a linear reciprocating motion to create joints, ideal for high-strength applications. Friction Stir Welding employs a rotating tool to stir and join materials at lower temperatures, offering superior mechanical properties and allowing for the welding of challenging materials like aluminum and magnesium.</p></p>
