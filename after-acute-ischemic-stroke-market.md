<p><p>The market for acute ischemic stroke treatments primarily includes antihypertensive therapeutics, antiplatelet therapeutics, and anticoagulants. Antihypertensive medications target high blood pressure to reduce stroke risk and complications. Antiplatelet drugs prevent blood clots by inhibiting platelet aggregation, helping to safeguard against further strokes. Anticoagulants work by interfering with blood coagulation pathways, reducing the likelihood of clot formation. Together, these therapies play critical roles in post-stroke management to improve patient outcomes and reduce recurrence rates.</p></p>
