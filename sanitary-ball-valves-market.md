<p><p>The sanitary ball valves market features two primary types of connections: clamp connections and weld connections. Clamp connections utilize a simple mechanism allowing easy assembly and disassembly, facilitating maintenance and cleaning in sanitary applications. They are widely used in food and beverage industries. On the other hand, weld connections provide a permanent, secure fit, ensuring a leak-free seal ideal for high-pressure environments. Both types are crucial for maintaining hygienic conditions in various processes, including pharmaceuticals and biotechnology.</p></p>
