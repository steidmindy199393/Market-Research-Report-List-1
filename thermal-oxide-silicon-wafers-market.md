<p><ul><li>Dry Thermal Oxide</li><li>Wet Thermal Oxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal oxide silicon wafers are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, created through oxidation processes. The market comprises two main types: dry thermal oxide and wet thermal oxide. Dry thermal oxide is formed in a high-temperature environment using oxygen, producing a thinner, denser layer with superior electrical properties, ideal for high-performance applications. Wet thermal oxide, on the other hand, is generated using steam, resulting in a thicker, less dense layer suitable for devices requiring robust insulation or moisture barriers.</p></p>
