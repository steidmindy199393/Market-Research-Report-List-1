<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic gate operator market is primarily divided into mechanical and hydraulic types. Mechanical operators utilize gears, motors, and chains to facilitate gate movement, offering reliability and straightforward installation for lighter gates. In contrast, hydraulic operators employ fluid pressure to drive the gate, providing greater power and smooth operation, particularly suited for heavier or larger gates. Both types enhance security and convenience, allowing for automated access control while catering to different functional and operational requirements.</p></p>
